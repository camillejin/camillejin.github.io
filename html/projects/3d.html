3D Music House

“You are Home” was an individual final project for Computer Graphics, a course I took in Spring, 2022. The class covered the basics of graphic programming and OpenGL, including topics such as shaders, transformation, texture, lighting, keyboard inputs, and model loading. In addition to applying the techniques taught in class, I further explored the use of audio libraries and Blender program to enhance the quality of the project.

The project was carried out when Harry Styles just released his new album “You are Home”. While listening to the music and looking at the album cover, I imagined myself in the room on the album art. The project started from this. The finished program allows users to enter and look around the room using keyboard inputs. You can pause or play music using space bar. For my program, royalty-free music was used instead of the music in the actual album. In the future, I hope to further explore the possibilities of this project. Collaboration with musicians or artists would be a dream come true!
