Policy Analysis -
MidLife Employment Center

Motivation

This policy analysis was conducted by five undergraduate students majoring in Economics.
The report was for the competition hosted by the National Strategy Institue and sponsored by Korea Institue of Public Finance.
We selected our subject, MidLife Employment Center for four main reasons -
1) increasing number of middle-aged population and their increasing demand for jobs,
2) poor access to employment centers and the lack of professionalism among their employees,
3) the budget needs to be scrutinized as the employment rate via the center has been decreasing,
4) small and mediu-sized company's participation in the educational programs also decreased.
