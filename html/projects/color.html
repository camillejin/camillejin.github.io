Color Design

Class Project
Timeline: Sep 2022 - Dec 2022
Tools: Gouache, KS195 Colored Paper, color pencil, Adobe Photoshop

These are some of the courseworks of Color Design, a class I took as an undergraduate at Yonsei University. The course contents include color theory, Munsell color system, color phenomenon, color analysis and more.




Color Cube

Light and dark side of the cube is painted using the tints and shades of the two colors. A tint is a mixture of a color with white, while a shade refers to a mixture with black.

Complementary Color Mixing

11x9 grid of mix between the two complementary colors, red and green.

Visual Memory

Painted without seeing, the squares in the centers represent my visual memory of the signature colors of the three brands, Coca Cola, Oreo, and Starbucks. For comparison, the squares on the right are painted after seeing the actual colors. I was able to see how impactful colors can be when used for branding.

Refinement Color Mixing

In this exercise, three different versions of Picasso's work is painted on a Bristol paper. Top right was painted using full color palette, bottom left with monochrome(green), and bottom right using the two complementary, red and green.
Original painting: Two Women Running on the Beach (The Race), 1922 by Pablo Picasso

Color Creation

This is an interactive emblem that I created to represent myself. The small star, circle and square can be moved around by dragging them with mouse.
