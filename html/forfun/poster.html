Poster Design

Inspiration

This poster is designed for an early Christmas party that I hosted.
The party “We Are Almost There” celebrates the end of something ahead of time.
I tried to capture this premature attitude and playfulness of the party through the bright and tinted background colors.
The colors were chosen to remind people of the Christmas tree along with the stair-shaped rectangles.
They also represent the front-view of the building where the party is held.



The building takes significance in this party as I,
the host of this party have lived in the house that existed prior to this new building.
My mother, who owns the building also have lived in the same house since she was a child.
The design shows the mixed sentiments and hopes I have regarding this construction.
The QR code on the top of the Christmas tree is both for the top ornament and RSVP.
