3D Music House

Motivation

“You are Home” is an individual final project for the course Computer Graphics which I took in Spring, 2022. The class covered the basics of graphic programming and OpenGL, including topics such as shaders, transformation, texture, lighting, keyboard inputs, and model loading. In addition to applying the techniques taught in class, I further explored the use of audio libraries and Blender program to enhance the quality of the project. The project was carried out when Harry Styles just released his new album “You are Home”. While listening to the music and looking at the album cover, I imagined myself in the room. The project started from this. The finished program allows users to enter and look around the room using keyboard inputs. In my program, royalty-free music was used instead of the music in the actual album. You can pause or play music using space bar.
