Your Library

Timeline: May 2020 - Jul 2020
Tools: Adobe Audition

Podcast production.

“Your Library” is a podcast produced in 2020. The podcast is both available in Korean and English. Full episodes are available on Naver Audioclip, Instagram, Apple Podcast, etc. I was actively involved with the overall production, process and my involvements vary from audio engineering to narration, content management, and translation! This project was a collaborative work of 5 members. Our guests ranged from middle school students to Professor of EcoScience Choi Jae Cheon.
