Tutu

Individual Project
Timeline: Summer 2022
Tools: Swift, SwiftUI, MVVM, CoreData, XCode, Firebase

“Tutu" is a mobile app developed to help freelance tutors with student&class management.

“Tutu” is an iOS application that helps freelance tutors to manage their classes and to connect with parents/guardians. This was an individual project that I carried out in my free time. I got the inspiration for the project from my personal experience as an online/offline tutor.

One of the things I noticed in my tutees(especially the younger ones) are that they learn the better when the instructor and parents communicate more often. This might be because tutees recall, talk about, or utilize the things I taught them outside the classroom when they spend time with their parents. Also, parents would know better how to complement the lessons. Hence this app was devised to aid the communication between the parent and the instructor such as sharing the lesson plans and class reports. The app also has functions to manage lesson schedules of different students using different colors.

This is a signup page for the app. I used Firebase Authentification to implement sign up/ sign in function.

The home tab shows scheduled lessons for the day. Tapping on the lesson item will display a sheet showing the details of the lesson.

The lesson detail view includes basic information such as class hour, student, lesson plans, and memo. The contents may be edited using the edit button.

Second tab is a calendar view that shows all lessons in a month. The color of the lesson block is the color of the student for the lesson.

The third tab shows the students that the tutor registered in their classroom. Tapping on the student block shows their details as well as lesson history.

As one of my first individual mobile app development project, I tried to implement a lot of knowledges and skills that I acquired from the Stanford 193p “Developing Apps for iOS” lecture including MVVM architecture, and Core Data.
