Tutu

Group Project
Timeline: 2022 - 2021
Tools: Swift, SwiftUI, MVVM, CoreData, XCode, Firebase

“Tutu" is a mobile app developed to help freelance tutors with student&class management.

“Tutu” is an iOS application that helps freelance tutors to manage their classes and to connect with parents/guardians. This was an individual project that I carried out in my free time. I got the inspiration for the project from my personal experience as an online/offline tutor. One of the things I noticed in my tutees(especially the younger ones) are that they learn the better when the instructor and parents communicate more often. This might be because tutees recall, talk about, or utilize the things I taught them outside the classroom when they spend time with their parents. Also, parents would know better how to complement the lessons. Hence this app was devised to aid the communication between the parent and the instructor such as sharing the lesson plans and class reports. The app also has functions to manage lesson schedules of different students using different colors.

As one of my first individual mobile app development project, I tried to implement a lot of knowledges and skills that I acquired from the Stanford 193p “Developing Apps for iOS” lecture including MVVM architecture, and Core Data.
